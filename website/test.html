Mission

The Computational Biology and Medical Informatics Reproducibility Network promotes reproducible research practices, open science, transparent reporting, and sustainable research software.

We bring together researchers, clinicians, software developers, institutions, publishers, funders and policymakers to improve research quality and strengthen confidence in computational scientific findings.
